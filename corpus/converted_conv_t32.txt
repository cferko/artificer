The Mark of Passage bestows magical powers related to
transportation and teleportation.
House: The humans of House Oraite are the bearers
of the Mark of Passage. They control a worldwide Couriers
Guild that uses its powers to carry parcels, messages, and
people over great distances instantaneouslyfor a high
price. The house has holdings and operates across Caeros,
though it traces its origins to Aundair. The house's
Transportation Guild oversees lightning rail and caravan
routes throughout central Caeros.
Unmarked members of House Oraite work as mundane
couriers, often within the boundaries of a single city
or nation
