The Mark of Warding protects places from unwanted
intruders and items from theft.
House: The dwarves of House Kundarak carry the
Mark of Warding. They control the Warding Guild,
which specializes in security for businesses and precious
goods. House Kundarak originated in the Mror Holds
and now has interests in many large towns and cities
throughout Khorvaire.
The unmarked members of House Kundarak work
as security consultants, often specializing in trapmaking
as well as running the House Kundarak bank.
